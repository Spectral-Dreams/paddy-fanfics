A comprehensive body count is yet to be provided by the Emirati authorities, but one shouldn't hold one's breath.

Just another day in Ersatz Dubai. The tall towers of glass and steel pierce the sky above and reflect the bright rays of the sun. Afternoon eventually gives way to evening and the sun begins its slow and steady descent.
As soon as that great ball of fire has set, the Kindred begin to stir awake from their day-sleep. Their Beasts, rife with insatiable Hunger, rouse them from their day-long slumbering.
Svetlana wakes up in her room. Her eyes pop wide open and she springs up from her bed. Her room feels very dry and stuffy, the fan above spins but does not provide any relief. It's quite hot inside. She forces some deep breaths.

The Assamite shakes her head a few times and puts her hands over her face, "No! No! No! Is in the past. I--I am safe here. They cannot hurt me, he--he cannot hurt me."
After a while, she stops trembling, takes a quick shower, and gets dressed. Tonight, she dresses for work and not combat. She puts on a knee-length bodycon dress, white flats, and a brown-colored scarf. The scarf is loosely draped around her neck, and the ends are wrapped around her left arm.
She brushes her hair a bit and makes sure that it's well-combed and neat. She needs the work more than anything else right now- John can meet with whoever to discuss the next steps to be taken against the Second Inquisition menace. Until then, she's gotta work and keep her head down.

She's gotta keep her Beast at bay. Her thoughts are far too muddled and grim to focus on anything EXCEPT for satiation of the bloodiest variety. Once she exits her apartment building, she spots a few potential candidates.
Her Hunger incessantly gnaws at her as she scans the crowd for an early(ish) dinner. After a while, she spies a young woman who is wearing a green hijab, a black blouse, and some baggy pants. Her brown skin is flushed pink and sweaty, and her clothes are stained with a bit of perspiration.

Blood fills the Neonate's hungry mouth. The eternal Beast within compels her to finish this poor mortal off, but Svetlana resists the inhuman urge and pulls away once she's taken what is required.
She drinks in the warmth of her victim's blood. It's been a while since she's last drank, and the feeling is rather comforting. This woman's blood is rather thick and has a tangy, salty taste.
Once done, the woman's eyes glaze over and her body goes limp. Svetlana licks the wound shut, checks for a pulse (faint but there), and leaves her near the dumpster. She props the woman's back against it and looks at her with a pained expression.
The Neonate softly whispers, "I am so sorry." She shakes her head and quickly leaves the woman behind.
