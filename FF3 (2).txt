Chapter 31: Beatdown
The sun- that great and terrible ball of light and fire- begins to rear its head in the horizon. Once it has risen, the Kindred in this part of the planet begin to fall to the irresistible pull of the day-sleep.
Markham's day-sleep is dark and dreamless. The entity within him whispers and muses on this and that, but she doesn't cause too great of a fuss.
For now.
Brock Davies rubs the sleep out of his eyes. He lets out a soft groan and mumbles as he climbs out of bed. He makes his way into the bathroom, splashes some water on his face, and checks himself out in the mirror.
He shoots finger guns at his sexy reflection, winks, and shaves off a few bits of stubble with a razor blade that is overdue for a changing.
While getting rid of some additional, unwanted facial hair, he is startled by a sudden knock on his bathroom door. He jumps out of his skin and cuts his cheek a little.
He yelps and curses under his breath, "For fuck's sake!"
Yumi, who is now staying with him, gasps lightly. She gulps and lowers her head when Brock yanks the door open. He looks at her and grimaces a bit.
Yumi apologizes and explains, "I--I didn't mean to startle you! I was just wondering where--where you keep the spare toothbrushes, that's all. I'm sorry."
The Ghoul of Dr. Collins sighs, "Huh. Well, you should've asked before barging into the bathroom like that, Yumi."
Yumi places a hand over her mouth, and her eyes widen when she sees how deep the cut is, "Oh my goodness! Your--your face! Are--are you OK? Do you need anything?"
Brock groans, "Never mind. I'll take care of it."
Yumi reassures him, "Your doctor buddy said that--that this is only temporary. Y'know, until I'm clean and able to find a job. Once I do that, I PROMISE to get outta your hair, mister."
Brock walks past her and murmurs, "I wouldn't bet on that happening anytime soon..."
"Huh?"
"Nothing. Never mind. Toothbrush, right? Yeah, I got a few. Medium or soft?"
Yumi grips her bicep nervously, "Anything's alright with me, Brock."
Brock's eyebrows go up, "Soft it is, then."
Yumi takes the toothbrush, but she still has a bewildered look all over her face, "You want some help with that cut?"
Brock shakes his head and wordlessly goes back to the bathroom. This time, he locks the door and goes about his business.
Yumi meanders around his place and stares out the window for a few minutes. The warmth of the early morning sun's rays feels nice on her skin. She blows a raspberry and hopes that she can finally get clean and free from Purdue's awful, terrible drug.
After a short while, Brock exits the bathroom. He walks past Yumi and makes his way to the fridge. He pulls out a can of OJ and takes a massive swig out of it.
Yumi sheepishly stares at him and inquires, "Is the sh--shower free?"
Brock almost spits out his drink, "Huh? Yeah, sure, go ahead. I uh...left you a change of clothes by the bathroom door."
Yumi presses her lips into a thin line and awkwardly shuffles past him. He finishes his drink, grabs some leftovers from yesterday, and looks out the window while chomping down on them.
A few minutes later, Yumi emerges from the bathroom, showered, and changed. After having lived in tattered tents and undignified squats for so goddamn long, to even have the things that others take for granted is enough to drive her to tears.
She's wearing a pair of Brock's joggers, an oversized checkered shirt, and a pair of his fluffy socks. She looks down at her feet and frowns.
Brock was initially reluctant on letting a complete stranger stay with him. But his Domitor explained the poor woman's circumstances to him.
Dr. Collins was pretty adamant on the fact that if Yumi were to go to an overcrowded shelter, she would no doubt end up in another bad situation and getting her clean would be nigh impossible.
And the word of the Domitor is often final.
Granted, a dope sick gal from off the streets staying with a gay man like Brock is certainly kinda awkward.
The neighbors would certainly say...stuff.
Mr. Davies, for his part, is not too terribly vexed about it. But he does, for obvious reasons, value his privacy. He was thinking of bringing a few cute guys over for some study (and snogging), but that will have to be put on hold.
For the time being, at least.
And Brock does feel kinda bad about Yumi's Oxy addiction situation. As such, turning her down or being mean to her would only serve to hurt her and would further complicate things.
Brock calls the mortal woman over and invites her to sit next to him. Yumi tentatively approaches and nervously licks her lips. She keeps her head lowered so as not to make eye contact.
Brock smiles and asks, "How was it? The shower?"
"It--it was nice. Af--after so long. It felt so nice, the way the warm water flowed down and got rid of all that accumulated grime and dirt, I just...I could have stayed in there forever. But I don't want to take too much of your time, mister."
Brock shrugs, "I got classes in an hour or so."
"That's nice. We didn't get much of a chance to chat ever since your doctor pal dropped me off over here. Is Dr. Collins your dad or something?"
Brock chuckles, "Him? Nah. But...eh...he is kinda the dad I never had. Long story. I wouldn't wanna bore you with all the details. Needless to say, he's been good to me when nobody else was."
Yumi looks away and her voice drops to a low whisper, "I know what you mean. He's a nice man. And what do you study?"
Brock runs his thumb over the band aid on his cheek, "I'm studying to become a plastic surgeon. I'm almost there. In another few weeks, I should finally be able to start seeing patients on my own. Wouldn't that be something?"
Yumi nods her head up and down, "Yeah. Yeah, it would be nice. Do you like what you do?"
"'Course. The human body fascinates me."
Yumi can feel her stomach growling. Even Brock hears it. He nods over to the fridge and tells her, "Grab whatever you like."
The mortal human briskly makes her way to the man's fridge and pulls it open. Her eyes widen in awe at how much food and beverages are in it.
Yumi is (sadly) no stranger to living off of garbage and scraps, but she is quite taken aback by the sheer variety of foods and drinks in Brock's fridge, which is ironic considering that there isn't much in the fridge to begin with.
Brock sees her reaction and smiles, "Go on, help yourself."
Yumi's baggy eyes fall upon a sandwich in a transparent rectangular plastic disposal box. She stares at it for a few moments and reaches into the fridge to grab them.
Brock shrugs a shoulder, "By all means. Don't worry. I...I understand. Go ahead. Need me to heat it up for you?"
Yumi shakes her head, "I--I know how to use an oven. Thanks."
Once her breakfast has been heated it (she might have gone a few seconds more than necessary in the oven), the 2 of them eat together in the living room.
She sits herself on the beanbag while Brock shoves her blanket aside and takes the couch.
He asks, "Is the couch OK for you?"
Yumi chomps on the sandwich and replies with an open mouth, "It's better than cardboard. But, yeah, it's great. Thank you."
Brock snorts, "Hmph. Well, it's the least I could do, considering all the shit that you've been through."
Yumi swallows and goes in for another large bite of the sandwich. She shrugs and says, "I haven't had a proper meal in--in days."
Brock's eyebrows shoot up and he winces slightly, "Damn."
From there, they share some idle chit chat and try to learn a little bit about each other. Brock still remains distant, but Yumi is ready to share absolutely everything about herself to anyone who'd even bother listening.
A few minutes go by. Yumi yawns and covers her mouth with her hand. Brock checks the time and prepares to head to the university. Papers to write, exams, classes, all the stuff that comes with the whole plastic surgeon territory.
He's so close now. In a few weeks' time, he'll finally get his hands on real patients and have the opportunity to do the things that he's always aspired to do.
Yumi can see the determination in the Ghoul's eyes. She profusely promises to not be a bother and stay in her lane, not make too much of a mess, and basically be on her best behavior.
Brock calms her down, "It's fine. And...uh...don't worry about it. Dr. Collins is a good man- you're in safe, capable hands. He knows his stuff."
He adds, "Just try not to get too comfy, alright?"
Yumi's cheeks flush, "Oh, no, it's--it's not like that. Once I'm clean, I get a job. Once I find that, I'll get my own place, and you'll be out of this. I--I don't wanna impose on you any longer than I have to."
"Thanks for the consideration, heh. Listen, I gotta head out now. Lemme just go over a few instructions with you before I leave..."
Throughout Brock's listing out the do's and don'ts of his digs, Yumi still has that wide-eyed and bewildered look all over her face. She nods and meekly listens to the Ghoul give out the rules of the house, as it were.
Yumi is so used to being kicked out of squats or having her "home" broken into or torn apart, or something along those lines, that to have a place which is actually nice and decent is enough to drive her to tears.
Once Brock has finished his lengthy list of instructions, he stands up and pats Yumi's back, gives her a tight smile, admonishes her to not go into his bedroom or break anything, and heads out into the warm, bright, and sunny day.
As he's walking, he thinks back to that news article he read about how The Tumbleweed (the gay bar he frequents) got raided by the FCPD recently.
(Knowing the FCPD, whatever the allegations were, you can bet that they likely hold no water- none whatsoever.)
He clicks his tongue and picks up the pace, "Well, there goes my favorite hangout spot. Tch."
Home was the only place where Brock could bring a cute guy without any fear of repercussions. Now, he's got a roomie and she's an Oxy addict on the path to getting clean.
In a way, awkward. Right?
Mr. Davies rolls his eyes, "Shit. Well, she's not so bad. I just...hmph, never mind. I was kinda in her place not that long ago. And Doc Collins took a chance on me. Maybe I should...just put aside my silly quirks and--and also try my best to help her out, too."
He squints when looking up at the sky and shrugs, "Eh, what the hell. Gotta roll with the punches, huh?"
He hikes the straps of his backpack further up his shoulders, puts on his cheap sunglasses, and walks on, whistling the tune to some cheesy love song.
Still, as Brock walks along, a part of him does wonder why Dr. Collins chose to put Yumi in his care, for lack of a better word at the moment. To Brock, this feels a bit weird, all things considered.
Anyways, the Ghoul of the good doctor shrugs and mentally readies himself for some fairly intense lectures and whatnot.
As the Tzimisce Ghoul trudges along his usual routes, Janus (Lenny's Ghoul) sits in a car with tinted windows and watches from afar. He has a small camera with him, which he uses to snap a few pictures of Brock from a distance.
Janus smirks, "Right on time, Mr. Davies. Right on time...you're too predictable."
It would seem that Prince Antonio has even got his fellow Ventrue (and their Ghouls) spying on those who conspired against him. And Lenny outsourced the Ghoul-watching to his own Ghoul for reasons that are obvious.
Janus futzes around with the camera and zooms in on Brock as the he crosses a street. He snaps a pic, smirks, and peels out after a few moments.
This is not good.
The sun must rise. The sun must set.
The night chugs along much like the numerous vehicles that begin to clog up the roads once the sun has fully disappeared from view. The sky above darkens and a new night begins.
Dr. Collins slakes his ceaseless thirst with a blood bag from his stash. He's dressed in a tucked-in green shirt, navy-blue pants, and an off-black blazer.
He smells of light cologne, but not so much that he's being utterly obnoxious about it. His eyes are deader than ever. He simply sits back and reads through the newspaper, stifling a few groans when his eyes flit over the celebrity sections.
A few minutes go by. The Stoker lets the fresh blood course its way throughout his Unliving body.
For tonight, his plans involve seeing a few (only a few) patients at the clinic and then joining Roxanne so that the both of them can meet up with Clive and try and get the other Brujah on board with the plan to depose the current Prince.
"It's a long shot," the Tzimisce GP murmurs.
He's somewhat hopeful about getting Clive and his merry band of Punks on board with this plan. He's also hoping that Roxanne (being a Brujah herself) can help sway Clive to their side.
Either way, he needs to put in his best foot forward so that the other Brujah don't see him as a threat or a burden. As it is, their "relationship" with Prince Antonio is pretty frayed.
A few hours later...
Wesley is told to take the remainder of the night off. His eyes widen in surprise. He quickly arranges the last few patient cards, turns off the aging computer, and does a few last-minute checks before thanking the alien-vampire doctor and hurrying away.
Once he's done with everything that he needs to do, he goes on home.
Dr. Collins watches his mortal receptionist leave. He huffs a bit, sniffs, and looks up at the clock. An elderly man approaches the doctor and apologizes for not making his initial appointment as he got the times a bit mixed up.
The Tzimisce sighs softly and ushers the elderly man inside- he's never been the type of buster to turn down a person in need, and this patient definitely is in need, if his shaking and slightly bent-over frame is anything to go by.
The elderly man sits quietly while the doctor flips the light switches on and goes to the filing cabinet to search for the proper patient card.
Brock raps his knuckles on the door and pokes his head in, "Oh...? Hi? Doc? You still in?"
Dr. Collins drops what he's doing, tells the patient that he'll be right back, and takes his Ghoul somewhere private.
The Stoker gulps and asks, "I need a favor from you, Brock."
The Ghoul's eyes are a tad baggy. His movements are also quite sluggish- a long and intense day at uni today! Brock yawns loudly and mumbles, "Hmmgrhfh. Sure. Name it...and I--I'll do it."
Dr. Collins hands over his car keys to the young man, "I'm gonna need you to pick Roxanne up and bring her here. She's somewhere on Hugo and 6th. You know how she looks. Bring her here. We...we're moving forward with our plans."
Brock side-eyes his Domitor, "Y'know, you haven't exactly looped me in on everything."
Dr. Collins puts his hand on his Ghoul's slumped shoulder, "We've been talking and it's been decided that Roxy's the one who should take Praxis. If all goes well, she's gonna be the Prince of Flimont City."
Brock's jaw drops, "Holy shit. Whoa! J--just like that?!" his tone does reflect his sheer surprise and disbelief.
"We're done sitting on our hands and waiting for Antonio to make his move. We need to act fast. If all goes well, then..." the doctor trails off and remembers that he has a patient to get back to.
Brock yawns yet again, "OK...sure. Sure thing. Just pick her up and bring her here, right? Where are you going, anyway?"
"To get support from the other Brujah in this Domain. We're hoping that a man like Clive can be reasoned with."
Brock's eyebrows shoot up and he faintly snickers, "Well. I'll leave that to you." He turns on his heel and trudges out of his Domitor's clinic, yawning once more.
Brock gets in the good doctor's goofy green car, inserts the key into the ignition, and starts it up. The engine purrs to life and the soon-to-be plastic surgeon is on the street and cruising towards Hugo and 6th.
He drives slowly and does a quick turn onto a smaller street, hoping to grab himself a large cup of coffee from a nearby coffeeshop. He spots a few that are open at this hour of the night.
He decides that Roxy wouldn't mind waiting for a few minutes longer, so he stops the car and enters this place.
The establishment in question is fairly small. The orange lighting is dim, and almost all of the seating is occupied by the kinds of people who work late at nights.
Hush-hush conversations over steaming cups of joe, cheap cigarettes, and the strong scents of the late-night office crowd pervade the air. Brock sneezes a few times and orders a single large cup of black coffee, extra sugar.
The baggy-eyed barista nods and gets the Ghoul's order ready.
As he waits for his coffee, Brock feels eyes boring into him. He turns and locks eyes with a few well-dressed dudes who are sitting in the corner. The topmost buttons of their shirts are open, and their hair is gelled back to near perfection.
Brock sighs, shrugs, thinks nothing of it, and pays for his order. He takes a sip; the taste makes its way all over his mouth and stings his taste buds. He can feel himself getting jolted awake.
From there, he makes his way back to his Domitor's car. He peels out and drives down the street, which is rather quiet, with the exception of the busy street just a few blocks ahead of him.
After effortlessly wading through some traffic, Brock releases some pressure from off the gas pedal and slows down a bit. The road ahead is somewhat dark. Sparse grassland can be seen on either side of the road.
He takes another sip from his steaming cup and lets his eyes rove about. He gulps and keeps on driving. The occasional speeding bike swooshes past him and it's loud enough to make his ears hurt a bit.
He squints and grimaces but does his best to keep his eyes on the road and his mind clear.
While driving along, he holds back a few more yawns. For a quick moment, he takes his hand off the steering wheel and takes a swig from his coffee cup.
It's nowhere near empty and it is still quite hot. As it happens, while he's drinking, he notices that a few beams of light have fallen upon him, and he squints against them.
They're coming from his right. Before he's even able to process what's going on, the beams of light grow brighter and bigger. His eyes widen in shock, and he lets out a fearful gasp.

They kick him around, punch him in the face, and break a few bones. A guy from that coffee shop earlier tosses a bat towards Lenny. He catches it and begins to beat upon the Ghoul, smashing the poor man's body repeatedly with the bat.
Brock lets out a bloody gargle and a wail, and his limp arms fall onto the road, skittering about. His eyes are glazed over. Lenny and the Ventrue goons begin to kick the already-mangled body as hard as they can.
Other Ventrue (or their Ghouls) put on some brass knuckles and hit the man's chest and face with them. 1 guy even beats his head in with a bent crowbar.
"YEEEEEAH!"

The Ventrue Ghoul scurries out from behind a car, "Yes?"

Poor Brock can barely breathe. His vision swims and is horribly blurry. He gasps and wheezes for air, but Lenny firmly steps on his neck and tells him, "Shhh. Relax. Breathe through your what's left of your nose and get some air in you. Hahahahaha!"

Janus returns with some stuff and reports, "Nothing else apart from insurance papers and lottery tickets from decades ago. Should we torch the ride?"
Leonard shrugs callously, "Sure. Just wait till we're out of here."
